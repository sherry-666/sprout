Design preview of Sprout's GraphQL API. Replaces the current REST surface (/api/*) with a single graph endpoint at /graphql. Errors-as-data unions are used for expected, recoverable failures (auth, invitations, validation); GraphQL errors[] is reserved for unauthorized access and bugs.
